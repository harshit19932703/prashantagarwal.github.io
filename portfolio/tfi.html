Cross Platform Attendace App

Description

During my internship with Teach For India, I worked on a cross platform mobile app for taking the attendace of students. This app was developed using HTML5, CSS3 and Jquery using Phonegap. For frontend Ionic framework was used.

Features

  • Uses Google OAUTH 2.0 for authentication.
  • Mark attendance of students
  • Automatically marks attendance of the fellow along with his current GPS coordinates.
  • Share classroom experience on Social media platforms.
  • Displays past 5 attendance of the student along with net attendance in percentage

Technology

  • HTML 5
  • CSS 3
  • MongpoDb
  • Phonegap
  • Jquery
  • Javascript
  • Ajax
